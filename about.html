About Our Agency

Dedicated to national security since 2005

Our History

Founded in response to growing security threats, our agency has been at the forefront of counter-terrorism efforts for nearly two decades. We've evolved from a small task force to a comprehensive national security organization with international reach.

2005

Agency Founding

Established with 50 agents and a single office in the capital.

2010

National Expansion

Opened regional offices in all major cities across the country.

2015

International Partnerships

Formalized collaborations with 12 foreign intelligence agencies.

2020

Cyber Division

Launched dedicated cyber-terrorism prevention unit.
