How to Recognize Suspicious Activity

Being aware of potential threats is the first step in preventing terrorism. Here are some signs to watch for:

  • Surveillance: Someone recording or monitoring activities, taking notes, using cameras, maps, or binoculars near key facilities
  • Elicitation: People or organizations attempting to gain information about military operations, capabilities, or people
  • Tests of Security: Attempts to measure reaction times to security breaches or to penetrate physical security barriers
  • Acquiring Supplies: Purchasing or stealing explosives, weapons, ammunition, etc.
  • Suspicious Persons: People who don't seem to belong in the workplace, neighborhood, business establishment, or anywhere else

If you see something, say something. Your report could save lives.
